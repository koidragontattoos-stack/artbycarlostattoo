Before You Book

What to include in your inquiry

What information helps the most?

The style you want, placement and rough size, reference images, and whether it's new work, a continuation, or a cover-up. Budget and timeline help too.

How do consultations work for out-of-state or international clients?

Remote consultation first — we discuss the design over email or a call, finalize the concept, then book your session dates so everything is ready when you arrive in Utah.

Is there a deposit?

Deposits and pricing are confirmed at the consultation, since every custom piece is scoped individually based on size, detail and sessions needed.
