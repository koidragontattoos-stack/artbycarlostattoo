About

Carlos Barrera

“Elevating your story through ink.”

Tattooing since 2012, Carlos Barrera has built a reputation for technical precision and creative vision across Japanese, realism, traditional, neo-traditional, fine line, floral, watercolor and bio-organic work.

His work is defined by sharp attention to detail, clean lines, and a deep understanding of how tattoos age. Every design is custom-created for the individual — composed for your anatomy, your story, and the decades ahead.

Carlos is a resident artist at Koi Dragon Tattoos in Murray, Utah — a 4.9★ rated studio with 503+ reviews — and takes bookings from clients traveling worldwide.
